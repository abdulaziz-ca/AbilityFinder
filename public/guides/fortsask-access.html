Fort Saskatchewan Access for Everyone

Recreation and transit support for eligible Fort Saskatchewan residents with limited income or approved income support.

What it is

Fort Saskatchewan’s Access for Everyone Program combines recreation support with the Everyone Rides transit subsidy for eligible residents.

Who it is for

The City lists a fully discounted annual multi-facility membership and an Everyone Rides transit subsidy. It also accepts several approved income-support programs; confirm your route when applying.

Amount or value

Free multi-facility membership + reduced local or commuter transit fares

How to apply

  1. Review the City’s current eligibility routes and application instructions.
  2. Apply with proof of limited income or the approved income-support document that applies to you.
  3. Ask about both the recreation membership and Everyone Rides transit subsidy.

What you may need

  • Proof of Fort Saskatchewan residence
  • Proof of income or approved income support

Practical tips

  • The City lists AISH, CPP Disability, FSCD and Income Support among the approved-program routes; check the current page for full details.
  • Ask whether your local or commuter transit trips qualify before buying a pass.

Timing

Ask when you apply.
