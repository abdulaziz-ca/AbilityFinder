Spruce Grove Low Income Transit Pass

A reduced local or commuter transit pass for qualifying Spruce Grove-area residents.

What it is

A City of Spruce Grove subsidized transit pass. Qualifying adults can buy a local pass for $25 a month or a commuter pass for $50 a month.

Who it is for

The City accepts applications year-round. The program also serves eligible Stony Plain and Parkland County residents.

Amount or value

$25/month local or $50/month commuter transit pass

How to apply

  1. Read the City’s current eligibility rules and application instructions.
  2. Apply with proof of address and household income.
  3. Choose the local or commuter pass that matches your trips.

What you may need

  • Proof of address
  • Proof of household income

Practical tips

  • The City says applications are accepted year-round.
  • Stony Plain and Parkland County residents should confirm the local address rules on the City page before applying.

Timing

Applications are accepted year-round.
