ICBC Disability Discount (Basic Autoplan)

A quarter off the basic part of your vehicle insurance. It comes with registering for the provincial fuel tax refund program, so the two go together.

Who it is for

Register for bc-fuel-tax-refund-disabilities first, then talk to an Autoplan broker about applying the discount to your policy.

Amount or value

25% off ICBC basic Autoplan insurance, including electric vehicles

Official information

  • Start with the fuel tax refund program
