ICBC Disability Discount (Basic Autoplan)

A quarter off the basic part of your vehicle insurance. It comes with registering for the provincial fuel tax refund program, so the two go together.

What it is

The discount is applied through an Autoplan broker after the Ministry of Finance registers you in the Fuel Tax Refund Program for Persons with Disabilities.

Who it is for

Register for bc-fuel-tax-refund-disabilities first, then talk to an Autoplan broker about applying the discount to your policy.

Amount or value

25% off ICBC basic Autoplan insurance, including electric vehicles

At a glance

Difficulty
Easy
Application
Fuel-tax registration, then ask an Autoplan broker
Decision timing
At policy transaction

How to apply

  1. Complete the provincial Fuel Tax Refund Program registration
  2. Wait for the Ministry of Finance registration confirmation
  3. Bring the confirmation and vehicle information to an Autoplan broker
  4. Ask the broker to apply the disability discount to eligible Basic Autoplan coverage

What you may need

  • Fuel Tax Refund Program registration confirmation
  • Vehicle registration and current Autoplan policy information
  • Identification requested by the broker

Practical tips

  • The discount is on Basic Autoplan, not every optional coverage charge
  • Ask the broker when the discount will take effect if the policy is already active
  • Electric vehicles can receive the insurance discount even though they do not use fuel

Timing

Ask an Autoplan broker after provincial registration is confirmed
