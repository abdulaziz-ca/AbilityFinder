TaxiSaver Program (TransLink)

Half-price taxi coupons for HandyCard holders, for trips where a scheduled HandyDART ride does not work.

What it is

TaxiSavers are coupon books for ordinary participating taxi trips. They are separate from taxis dispatched by HandyDART as part of a booked HandyDART trip.

Who it is for

Order by cheque or money order with a self-addressed envelope, or buy in person at the TransLink Customer Service Centre at Waterfront Station, which takes cash, debit or credit. Buying in person, you or your representative must present the original HandyCard.

Amount or value

Half-price taxi coupons — $25.00 buys one book with $50.00 worth of coupons, maximum two books per month

At a glance

Difficulty
Easy
Application
HandyCard first, then order coupons
Decision timing
After HandyCard approval

How to apply

  1. Get and keep an active TransLink HandyCard
  2. Choose mail order or in-person purchase at the TransLink Customer Service Centre at Waterfront Station
  3. For mail order, send a cheque or money order with the required self-addressed envelope
  4. For an in-person purchase, bring the original HandyCard; a representative may purchase for you if they bring it
  5. Show the HandyCard to the taxi driver and use coupons toward the participating taxi fare

What you may need

  • Original TransLink HandyCard
  • Cheque or money order for mail orders, or accepted payment for an in-person purchase

Practical tips

  • One $25 book contains $50 in coupons and the limit is two books per month
  • You can use post-dated cheques to pre-order future months
  • Call before travelling to Waterfront Station if you need accessibility or current counter-hour information

Timing

In person is immediate when stock and eligibility are confirmed; mail delivery varies
