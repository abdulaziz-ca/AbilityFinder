Metro Vancouver · Transit

TaxiSaver Program (TransLink)

Half-price taxi coupons for HandyCard holders, for trips where a scheduled HandyDART ride does not work.

Who it is for

Order by cheque or money order with a self-addressed envelope, or buy in person at the TransLink Customer Service Centre at Waterfront Station, which takes cash, debit or credit. Buying in person, you or your representative must present the original HandyCard.

Amount or value

Half-price taxi coupons — $25.00 buys one book with $50.00 worth of coupons, maximum two books per month

At a glance

Difficulty
Easy
Application
HandyCard first, then order coupons
Decision timing
After HandyCard approval

How to apply

  1. Open the official program page and review the current eligibility rules, dates and application method.
  2. Gather the documents named on the official application page; requirements can change.
  3. Apply through the official link below, then keep a copy or confirmation number for follow-up.
