Cochrane Connect Card

A City card for residents facing financial or situational barriers, with local transit and recreation discounts.

What it is

The Cochrane Connect Card gives qualifying residents 25% off COLT monthly and 10-ticket transit passes, and 50% off SLS Centre 30-day and monthly passes.

Who it is for

Cochrane assesses financial and situational need. Book an appointment and bring the requested income and address documents.

Amount or value

25% off local transit + 50% off SLS Centre monthly passes

How to apply

  1. Book the appointment described on the City’s Connect Card page.
  2. Bring proof of income and your Cochrane address.
  3. Ask which current transit and SLS Centre discounts the card gives you.

What you may need

  • Proof of income
  • Proof of Cochrane address

Practical tips

  • This is not only an income test; the City also describes situational need.
  • Confirm the current eligible passes at your appointment before purchasing.

Timing

Ask when booking the appointment.
