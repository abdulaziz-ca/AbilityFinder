Surrey · Recreation

Leisure Access Program (LAP)

Surrey's recreation subsidy. Children, youth and seniors get a free year-long pass to every City recreation facility; adults get three quarters off.

What it is

Surrey accepts several qualifying routes. Income-tax applicants must meet both the family-income table and the additional investment, rental, RRSP-deduction and capital-gain limits on the current City page.

Who it is for

Enrolment is valid for one year. If you apply with income tax documents, the family net income maximum (line 23600 of your Notice of Assessment) is $25,588 for a family of 1, $31,140 for 2, $38,777 for 3, $48,377 for 4, $55,089 for 5, $61,095 for 6, $67,099 for 7, and $6,006 more for each additional person.

Amount or value

Free 1-year pass for ages 0-18 and 60+, or 75% off for adults 19-59, plus 75% off registered courses

How to apply

  1. Review the City's qualifying routes and select the one that applies
  2. Complete the Leisure Access Program application and copy the documents required for that route
  3. Submit online and wait for staff to schedule an appointment, or submit in person at a listed Surrey facility
  4. After approval, use the pass through the MySurrey account or call registration staff

What you may need

  • Application form
  • Government-issued photo identification for one adult
  • Proof of Income Statement or Notice of Assessment for each adult when applying by income
  • Program confirmation, DTC information or referral documents for another qualifying route

Practical tips

  • Translation and interpretation support are available free
  • Check that tax documents are from a currently accepted year before submitting
  • Ask whether a specialty program is excluded before registering

Timing

Online applicants are contacted for appointments on a first-come, first-served basis
