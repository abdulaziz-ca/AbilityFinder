Okotoks Recreation Fee Assistance

A recreation-fee subsidy for qualifying low-income Okotoks and Foothills County residents.

What it is

The Town’s Recreation Fee Assistance Program helps qualifying residents afford most Town recreation programs, passes and admission at an 80% discount.

Who it is for

The Town describes 80% support for most Town programs, passes and admission. Check current exclusions and camp limits before registering.

Amount or value

80% off most Town programs, passes and admission

How to apply

  1. Read the Town’s current program information and eligibility rules.
  2. Apply with the income and residence documents requested.
  3. Check the program period and any limits before registering for an activity.

What you may need

  • Proof of income
  • Proof of Okotoks residence

Practical tips

  • The Town also serves eligible Foothills County residents; confirm the current boundary rule if that is you.
  • Ask about exclusions before choosing a camp or program.

Timing

Ask when you apply.
