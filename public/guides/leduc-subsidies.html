Leduc Transit & Recreation Subsidies

Financial-navigation support that can reduce local, commuter and LATS transit costs and provide recreation access.

What it is

Leduc Family and Community Support Services can help qualifying residents access reduced transit and recreation costs as part of its housing and financial-navigation support.

Who it is for

Leduc lists half-price local, commuter and LATS transit passes, plus a free annual Leduc Recreation Centre membership for qualifying residents.

Amount or value

Half-price transit + a free Leduc Recreation Centre annual membership

How to apply

  1. Contact Leduc FCSS for a financial-navigation appointment.
  2. Ask about the transit and recreation subsidies when discussing your situation.
  3. Follow their directions for the documents needed to assess eligibility.

What you may need

  • Proof of Leduc residence
  • Financial information requested by FCSS

Practical tips

  • Ask specifically about LATS if you use specialized transit.
  • The City’s page describes an assessment, so do not assume eligibility until FCSS confirms it.

Timing

Ask FCSS when you contact them.

Official information
