Taxi Saver Program (BC Transit)

Half-price taxi coupons for permanently registered handyDART riders, for one-off trips when handyDART does not work.

Who it is for

Coupons come in $1, $2 and $5 values and taxi drivers do not give change for them — pay any remainder in cash. Coupons cannot be transferred or used as a tip. The $40 for $80 package and the one-per-month limit are confirmed for the Victoria region; other BC Transit regions run their own versions, so check your region's page.

Amount or value

Half-price taxi coupons — in the Victoria region, $40 buys an $80 package, one package per month
