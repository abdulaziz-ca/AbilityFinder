Kamloops · Recreation

ARCH (Affordable Recreation for Community Health)

Kamloops' recreation subsidy — and the same application also gets you KamPASS, the City's affordable transit program, so one form covers both.

What it is

The credits reduce the cost of fitness classes, activities, and facility memberships or drop-in services.

Who it is for

ARCH is an annual program and requires a new application each year. Applying for KamPASS, the City's affordable transit program, is done through the same ARCH application — so do not apply separately.

Amount or value

Up to $400 in recreation credits per year
