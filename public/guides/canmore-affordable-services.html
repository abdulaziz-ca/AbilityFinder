Canmore Affordable Services Program

A Town program that provides qualifying residents with lower-cost local services, including transit and recreation.

What it is

Canmore’s Affordable Services Program provides qualifying residents with discounts on Town and community services, including local transit and recreation.

Who it is for

The Town lists discounts across local transit, recreation and community services. Transit discount tiers differ, so confirm the current rate for your household.

Amount or value

Discounts on local transit, recreation and community services

How to apply

  1. Review the Town’s current eligibility and application instructions.
  2. Apply with the income and residency documents requested.
  3. Ask which discounts apply to the services you use.

What you may need

  • Proof of income
  • Proof of Canmore residence

Practical tips

  • Transit discounts are tiered; the Town page is the source of truth for the current rate.
  • Apply before assuming a discount is available for a specific community service.

Timing
