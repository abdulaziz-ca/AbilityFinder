Metro Vancouver · Transit

HandyCard (TransLink)

Photo ID card for Metro Vancouver riders who cannot use conventional transit without assistance. It gets you concession fares, lets an attendant travel free, and unlocks the TaxiSaver program.

Who it is for

The attendant travels free on buses, SkyTrain, SeaBus and West Coast Express. HandyDART trips are paid separately at the 1-Zone fare.

Amount or value

Free photo ID card — concession fares for you, free travel for your attendant, and access to half-price TaxiSaver coupons
